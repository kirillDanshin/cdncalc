What is this project about?

CDNcalc is a simple calculator that allows you to choose between multiple CDNs and calculate an approximate monthly price

How exact are the final prices?

All pricing data should be correct to a certain point. To deal with complicate pricing per region we included optional settings that allow you to tweak your bandwidth and see the most accurate price possible. Also take in mind that all CDN companies offer special pricing for bandwidth of 30-50TB+

How can I help?

You can make changes to the website using the official github page. Also we can open issues with feedback and bug reports.
